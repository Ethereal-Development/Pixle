About




Inspired by Wordle, Pixle is a game made for anyone. It is fun, simple and easy for anyone to understand. Enjoy :)

Made by Ethereal Dev.

How to Play




At the top of the screen is a pixelated image. You must guess what it is.
With each guess, the image gets less and less pixelated. The guesses can be anything. It's up to you. After typing in a guess, press enter. Then, the guess will turn green or red depending on if you're right or wrong. Good Luck!
