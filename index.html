About




Inspired by Wordle, this is a game made for anyone. It is a fun, simple game that's easy for anyone to understand.

Made by Ethereal Dev.

How to Play




In the center of the screen there is a pixelated image. You must guess what this image is. With each guess, the image gets less and less pixelated. The guesses be any length and comprised of any letters, even if the word is nonsensical. Up to you what to guess!
